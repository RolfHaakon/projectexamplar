Mission

Membership

  • Recruit new members
  • Increase engagement of members in our activities, e.g. volunteering for administration, organising and promoting concerts and workshops

Concerts

  • Increase ticket sales, especially to the general public
  • Encourage performers to let us organise a concert for them
  • Encourage performers to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc...

Success Evaluation

New member recruited

Evaluated by the increase of new members over the past three months. It will be considered as successful should the new members quota reach the goal of 20 members.

Engagement of current members

Evaluated through increase of signs ups for voluntary activities such as organising and promoting concerts and workshops, as well as volunteering for administration. Considered as a success if over 30 members sign up for at least 2 acitivty per month

Ticket sales

Evaluated by increase of ticket sales to the public. Meeting the quota of 50 ticket sales per concert to the general public would be considered as a success.

Performers requesting concerts

Evaluated by increase of concerts requested. It will be considered as a success if there is an increase of concerts requested per month over 3 months

Performers cooperation

Evaluated by the number of performers agreeing to help with promotion, photo shoots, and media releases.

Target Audience

The typical audience the site targets are people with specialised taste. This include people of all ages and tastes.
